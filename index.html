Common Questions
Everything you need to know
What is vibe coding? +
Vibe coding is building software driven by intuition and creativity rather than rigid requirements. You code because it feels right, experiment freely, and let the process be as rewarding as the result.
Who can join VibeLab? +
Anyone who builds with feeling. Whether you're a beginner experimenting with your first project or a seasoned developer making side projects for fun — if you vibe while you code, you're one of us.
What kind of projects can I post? +
Anything creative. Web apps, generative art, browser games, CLI tools, AI experiments, interactive visualizations — if you built it and had fun doing it, share it.
Does my project need to be finished? +
Not at all. Half-finished experiments, early prototypes, and weird tangents are all welcome. We celebrate the process, not just the final product.
Is VibeLab free? +
Yes, completely free. VibeLab is a community project built for creators, by creators. No paywalls, no premium tiers.
How do I get started? +
Click "Share Your Vibe" to post your first project. Browse the community gallery for inspiration. Give vibes to projects you love. That's it — no complicated onboarding.
